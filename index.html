OUR MISSION

Accelerate science via intelligence augmentation

We want to enable researchers to have thoughts and connections they would not been able to have otherwise. Outsiders assume that scientific discoveries are obvious in retrospect because of the inherent assumption of perfect information (ex. ‘Since paper X and paper Y were out, Z should have been obvious’). We want to enable researchers to actually have more obvious connections.
